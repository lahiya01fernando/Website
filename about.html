About Us

Welcome to Ceylon Tea Distributer of Sri Lanka, where we bring you the finest selection of premium Ceylon tea. Our mission is to provide high-quality, authentic Ceylon tea that offers a delightful and refreshing experience with every sip.

Founded in 2020, Ceylon Tea Distributer was established out of a passion for showcasing the rich heritage of Ceylon tea and a commitment to supporting sustainable tea production. We work closely with local tea growers to ensure that every tea we offer meets our rigorous standards for quality and authenticity.

Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service and ensuring that your tea journey with us is both enjoyable and satisfying. Thank you for choosing Ceylon Tea Distributer of Sri Lanka!
